Ortogeksagonal yacheyka

Orthohexagonal cell (En). Ортогексагональная ячейка (Ru).

Geksagonal panjarada tanlanishi mumkin bo'lgan minimal hajmli rombik asosmarkazli yacheyka (C-yacheyka). Geksagonal yacheykaning a, b, c vektorlari va ortogeksagonal yacheykaning a', b, c' vektorlari a = a, b'= a + 2b, c' = c munosabat bilan bog'liq. Bundan b'= a kelib chiqadiki, va ortohekkonal yacheykaning hajmi geksagonal yacheykaning o'lchamidan ikki barobar ko'p.
